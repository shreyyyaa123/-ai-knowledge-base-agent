Employee Benefits Guide

Health Insurance:
- Full family coverage including spouse and 2 children
- Cashless treatment at 500+ network hospitals across India
- Annual health checkup for employee and family included
- Coverage amount: Up to 5 lakhs per year
- Maternity benefits: Up to 2 lakhs
- Pre-existing conditions covered after 2 years
- Mental health counseling: 10 sessions per year covered

Retirement Benefits:
- Company contributes 12% to Provident Fund (PF)
- Gratuity payable after 5 years of service
- National Pension Scheme (NPS) with 10% company contribution
- Voluntary Pension Fund option available
- Financial planning consultation provided annually

Financial Benefits:
- Performance bonus: Up to 3 months salary based on ratings
- Annual increment: 8-15% based on performance review
- Referral bonus: 50,000 rupees per successful hire
- Relocation assistance: Up to 1 lakh for inter-city transfers
- Festival bonus: One month basic salary during Diwali

Learning and Development:
- Learning budget: 50,000 rupees per year per employee
- Access to online courses (Coursera, Udemy, LinkedIn Learning)
- Conference attendance: Up to 2 conferences per year
- Certification reimbursement: 100% for job-relevant certifications
- In-house training programs and workshops
- Mentorship program with senior leaders

Work-Life Balance:
- Flexible working hours (core hours: 10 AM - 4 PM)
- Work from home: 2 days per week
- 5-day work week (Monday to Friday)
- Festival holidays: 12 paid holidays per year
- Birthday leave: One day off on your birthday
- Sabbatical option: 3 months after 5 years of service

Wellness Benefits:
- Free lunch and snacks in office cafeteria
- Gym membership reimbursement: 2,000 per month
- Yoga and meditation sessions twice a week
- Mental health support and counseling services
- Annual team outing and celebrations
- Sports facilities and equipment

Family Benefits:
- Childcare support: 5,000 per month for children under 5
- Education assistance: 25,000 per year per child
- Parental support for emergencies
- Elder care assistance program
