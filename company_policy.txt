Company Leave Policy

Annual Leave: 
- Employees get 20 days of paid leave per year
- Leave must be requested 2 weeks in advance through the HR portal
- Maximum 5 consecutive days without manager approval
- Unused leave can be carried forward up to 5 days to next year
- Leave encashment available for unused days at year end

Sick Leave:
- 10 days of sick leave per year
- Medical certificate required for more than 2 consecutive days
- Can be used for family medical emergencies (spouse, children, parents)
- Half-day sick leave is allowed with supervisor approval
- Emergency sick leave can be applied retroactively within 24 hours

Work From Home Policy:
- 2 days per week allowed for all employees
- Must be approved by manager 24 hours in advance
- Core hours 10 AM - 4 PM must be available online
- Mandatory office days: Monday and Friday for team collaboration
- Internet reimbursement provided for WFH days

Casual Leave:
- 7 days of casual leave per year
- Can be taken without prior approval for emergencies
- Must inform manager within 2 hours of absence
- Cannot be combined with other leave types
- No carry forward to next year

Maternity/Paternity Leave:
- Maternity leave: 26 weeks paid leave
- Paternity leave: 2 weeks paid leave
- Adoption leave: 12 weeks paid leave
- Extended unpaid leave available on request
